Our Mission


Since 1998, The FruitGuys has been on a mission to sustainable agriculture and combat hunger. Buying your fruit from The FruitGuys helps our family-owned and operated B Corp business sustain the small independent farmers we support and source from and the hunger-relief non-profits we donate to through our GoodWorks programs. We support this mission by donating a minimum value of 20% annual profits to fight hunger and support sustainable farming practices.
